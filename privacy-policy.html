Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Mulli Golf ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Location Information

  • What we collect: Your device's location when using the app
  • Why we collect it: To show golf courses near you and provide personalized recommendations
  • When we collect it: Only when you grant permission and actively use location-based features

Camera and Photos

  • What we collect: Photos you choose to take or select from your photo library
  • Why we collect it: To allow you to capture and share photos of your golf rounds and course experiences
  • When we collect it: Only when you actively choose to take or select photos within the app

Golf Course Data

  • What we collect: Your ratings, reviews, and preferences for golf courses
  • Why we collect it: To improve recommendations and help other golfers discover great courses
  • When we collect it: When you voluntarily rate or review courses

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide location-based golf course recommendations
  • Allow you to document your golf experiences with photos
  • Improve our service and course database
  • Enhance user experience through personalized content

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • Location data is only used for app functionality and is not stored permanently
  • Photos remain under your control and can be deleted at any time

Your Rights

  • You can disable location access at any time through your device settings
  • You can delete photos you've uploaded through the app
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • With your explicit consent
  • To provide essential app functionality (e.g., map services)

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and on this website.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@muligolf.com
  • Website: muligolf.com

This privacy policy ensures compliance with Apple App Store requirements and applicable privacy laws including GDPR and CCPA.
