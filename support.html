Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find golf courses near me?

Enable location services for Mulli Golf in your device settings, and the app will automatically show courses near your location.

Can I use the app without creating an account?

Yes! You can browse and discover courses without an account. However, to rate courses and save favorites, you'll need to create an account.

How do I rate a golf course?

After playing a course, open it in the app and tap the star rating. You can also add photos and write a review.

My location isn't working correctly

Make sure you've granted location permission to Mulli Golf in your device settings. If issues persist, try restarting the app.

How do I delete my account?

Contact us at the email below and we'll help you delete your account and associated data.
